Classic Matzo Ball Soup

The best matzo ball soup you'll ever have!

Over the years I have tried many different restaurant matzo ball soup recipes. With conviction I can say one thing; most of them are lackluster. Either the matzo balls are way too big, the chicken is springy, or the broth is watered down. Yet people tend to settle for these restaurant matzo ball soups because they think that it is too difficult to make it themselves. Well I'm here to tell you that making a high quality matzo ball soup is extremely easy. All it takes is finding the right ingredients and a bit of patience. I guarantee that once you try this recipe you'll never want to try another.

Estimated total time: 3 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of unpeeled carrots (about 10-15 carrots)
  • 8-10 unpeeled parsnips
  • 1 large bunch of celery
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 whole organic chicken
  • 1 fennel bulb with stalks
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper (fresh cracked if possible)
